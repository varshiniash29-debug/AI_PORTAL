Cyber Safety Guidelines for Defence Personnel

Common Cyber Threats

Be aware of these attacks targeting defence personnel

  • Phishing emails impersonating official defence communications
  • Fake government websites asking for login credentials
  • Social engineering via WhatsApp and social media
  • Malicious attachments in emails or messaging apps
  • Ransomware targeting official or personal devices
  • Financial fraud through UPI and online banking

Immediate Steps to Take

If you suspect a cyber incident

  • Do not click on suspicious links or open unknown attachments
  • Disconnect the affected device from the internet immediately
  • Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details
  • Screenshot and preserve evidence before deleting
  • Report the incident on this portal with full details
  • Inform your unit's security officer without delay
